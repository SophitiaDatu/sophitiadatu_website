Kultura/EN Barong: Islas Filipinas Collection

Inspired by Philippine festivals, each barong showcases how traditional craftsmanship can capture the essence of cultural celebration in modern, wearable art. The challenge was to use a straightforward visual approach that distinguishes five different festivals—Masskara, Ati-Atihan, Moriones, Higantes, and Panagbenga—at a single glance. Local indigenous textiles, intricate patterns, and bold colors from each festival were incorporated, with iconic symbols positioned as strong focal points.

Technical Execution
